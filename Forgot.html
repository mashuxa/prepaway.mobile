Retrieve Your Prepaway Account Password

Forgot your password? Not a big deal, it happens to everyone.

Just enter the email address you used during your registration process, and your password information will be sent to you. You will be able to change or update your password in the Members' Area once you have logged into your account.

You can also use this page to resend 'Account confirmation' e-mail message to your e-mail address.

NOTE: To make sure that you can receive the email from Prepaway.com, please add the "prepaway.com" domain to your email 'safe list' and add email address support@prepaway.com to your address book.

If you do not receive the email within 3 minutes, be sure to check your Spam or Junk Mail folders.
